## Overview

The **Court** role is for judicial officers who handle the final stage of the complaint lifecycle. After police have verified a complaint and, where applicable, the bank has processed the account freeze, the case becomes available to the court. Court officials review the full case file and record a judicial decision that either approves or rejects further action.

---

## Accessing the dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Go to `/login`, enter your credentials, and click **Sign In**. The application detects your role as `Court` and renders the Court dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a view">
    The menubar offers two views:

    - **Dashboard** — case files pending a court decision (default view)
    - **Status** — all cases reviewed by the court
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reviewing case files

The default dashboard view fetches all cases that have completed the preceding verification stages and are pending a court decision (`GET formupload/downloadformsnotverify`). Each case card displays the full complaint record:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Basic complaint details">
    - Application number (`applicationNo`)
    - Victim email address
    - Category (`category`) and subcategory (`subcategory`)
    - Incident date (`date`)
    - Delay in reporting (`delay`)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bank details (when money was lost)">
    Shown when `bankbool` is `true`:

    - Bank name (`bankname`), IFSC no. (`ifsc`), account no. (`accno`)
    - Transaction ID (`transactionID`), amount, reference no. (`refno`), transaction date
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email compromise details (when applicable)">
    Shown only for the **Business Email Compromise/Email Takeover** subcategory:

    - Email taken by (`emailtakenby`)
    - Full email header (`header`)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Incident location and proof">
    - Platform where the incident occurred (`incidentoccur`)
    - Suspect identifier (`whereid`)
    - Uploaded proof image — click to open the full-size version in a new tab
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Prior verification decisions">
    - **Our Prediction** (`legitment`) — the ML model's fraud score
    - **Police Verification** (`verifybypolice`) — the police officer's decision
    - **Bank Status** (`verifybybank`) — the bank's account freeze decision (shown if `verifybybank` is set)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recording a court decision

Each case in the pending queue has a **Court Decision** control at the bottom of its card.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the case file">
    Examine the full complaint record: the victim's reported details, proof image, ML fraud prediction, police verification, and bank freeze status.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a decision">
    Under **Court Decision**, select:

    - **Yes** — approve the case for further action (triggers the bank de-freeze workflow for cases where `bankbool` is `true`)
    - **No** — reject the case

    The radio input name is keyed to the application number: `verify_[applicationNo]`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the decision">
    Click **Submit**. The platform sends `POST formupload/verifybypolice` with the payload:

    ```json payload
    {
      "verifybypolice": "yes" | "no",
      "applicationNo": "[applicationNo]"
    }
    ```

    The case list refreshes after submission by re-fetching `GET formupload/downloadformsnotverify`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  A court decision of `yes` on a complaint with `bankbool = true` triggers the de-freeze request that appears in the Bank role's Defreeze queue. Victims can see the court decision reflected in their complaint status view as `verifybycourt`.
</Note>

## Court's position in the complaint lifecycle

The court acts as the final verification gate before de-freeze processing. The following table shows the full lifecycle and where the court fits:

| Stage | Role responsible | Field set |
|---|---|---|
| Complaint filed | Victim | — |
| ML fraud scoring | System | `legitment` |
| Police verification | Police | `verifybypolice` |
| Account freeze | Bank | `verifybybank` |
| Judicial decision | **Court** | **`verifybycourt`** |
| Account de-freeze | Bank | `defreezestatus` |

<Tip>
  Switch to the **Status** view to see all cases you have already decided, along with the downstream de-freeze status for cases you approved.
</Tip>
